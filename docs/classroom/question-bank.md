# Question bank

Use these as classroom prompts or GitHub issues. The best questions are small enough to answer in one page and concrete enough to test.

## Terminology and translation

- How do I decide whether two terms are synonyms or separate concepts?
- How do I document forbidden terms in a style guide?
- How do I export a bilingual glossary from a CAT tool?
- How do I compare two versions of a termbase?
- How do I add sources and reliability labels to terminology?
- How do I clean inconsistent language codes in a glossary?
- How do I build a termbase from student translations?
- How do I turn a terminology list into flashcards?

## Corpora and text analysis

- How do I build a mini corpus from ten texts?
- How do I remove boilerplate from web texts?
- How do I compare keywords between two small corpora?
- How do I make a concordance and interpret KWIC lines?
- How do I lemmatize a corpus for a language with rich morphology?
- How do I calculate type-token ratio without embarrassing myself?
- How do I find collocations and avoid overclaiming?
- How do I document corpus composition?
- How do I create a stop-word list for a specific genre?
- How do I compare human and AI writing styles?

## Archives, OCR, and editions

- How do I check whether a PDF already has text?
- How do I OCR a scanned page with columns?
- How do I correct OCR without losing the original?
- How do I transcribe a handwritten source diplomatically?
- How do I encode uncertain readings?
- How do I link a transcription to an image?
- How do I cite a digitised archival source?
- How do I make a five-object digital exhibit?

## Mapping and visualization

- How do I geocode historical place names?
- How do I map fictional places without pretending they are real?
- How do I make a timeline from biographical data?
- How do I visualize missing data?
- How do I make a network of characters in a novel?
- How do I avoid making a hairball network?
- How do I choose between a map, timeline, chart, and table?

## Audio, video, and multimodal data

- How do I transcribe a short interview?
- How do I create accessible subtitles?
- How do I compare automatic and corrected subtitles?
- How do I segment a podcast by topic?
- How do I create metadata for an oral-history recording?
- How do I make a sound map?

## AI and source criticism

- How do I make AI extract entities from a text and then audit them?
- How do I detect hallucinated citations?
- How do I compare two prompts for the same DH task?
- How do I use AI to draft metadata without trusting it blindly?
- How do I test whether an LLM handles Slovene dialect?
- How do I build a tiny retrieval-based chatbot from course documents?

## Publishing, reproducibility, ethics

- How do I choose a license for teaching materials?
- How do I write a dataset citation?
- How do I make a project reproducible without overengineering it?
- How do I decide whether sensitive data should be public?
- How do I write a takedown policy?
- How do I document known problems in a dataset?
- How do I create a GitHub issue for a missing answer?
